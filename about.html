About Us

Aso Surveys Ltd

ASO SURVEYS LTD is a Nigerian surveying firm that was founded on January 3, 2014 by Surv. Aziegbemhin Sylvester Oriazowan. The company was initially known as Accurate Digital Surveys before it was fully incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission on October 19, 2020.

The company has grown significantly over the years and now has a staff strength of over eight employees. Its headquarters is located at 1 Abadi Lane, Awka and is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment.

Aso Surveys Ltd is committed to being the most equipped surveying firm in Nigeria in terms of instrumentation and accuracy. The company aims to achieve this vision by providing first-class surveying services that will stand the test of time in terms of integrity and reliability using the best equipment and methods.

The company's area of expertise includes topographic surveys, cadastral surveys, engineering surveys, land in dispute surveys, and layout surveys. ASO ACCURATE DIGITAL SURVEYS NIG. LTD is dedicated to providing these services with the utmost professionalism and accuracy to meet the needs of its clients.

About The CEO

Surv. Aziegbemhin Sylvester Oriazowan

Surv. Aziegbemhin Sylvester Oriazowan was born in the early 90's into the family of Mr. & Mrs. Gregory Ikhuirionan Imhanfidon. He is a native of Ogbokpa Village, Ewwatto in Esan South East Local Government Area of Edo State.

He attended Ivie Nursery and Primary School, Uromi, Edo State.

He then proceeded to Inegbeboh Comprehensive College, Uromi, for his junior secondary education where he studied on local scholarship due to his academic performance.

He changed to UFUA Memorial Secondary School, where he graduated in 2006/2007 academic session as the best in English Language, Best in Social Sciences and the Overall best in Academic.

He got admitted into Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, Anambra State, in 2008 through their pre-science programme to study Surveying and Geo-informatics and was awarded a Federal Government Scholarship and he graduated in 2013 with First-class Honours in Surveying and Geo-informatics.

He further went to Obafemi Awolowo University where he got certified in Web Design.

He also registered with Institute of Customer Relationship Management where he did his Professional Postgraduate Diploma and became a Certified Customer Service Professional.

He further registered with Project Management Professional Institute and got certified as a Project Management Professional.

He also registered with Millennium Training Acquisition Limited and became certified in Health Safety and Environment Level I, II, III.

Surv. Aziegbemhin Sylvester is Registered with SURVEYORS' COUNCIL OF NIGERIA as a Registered Land Surveyor and is Licensed to practice as a Land Surveyor anywhere in Nigeria.
